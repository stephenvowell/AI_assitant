PRISM Marketing Sheet 2020

Global Internet of Things (IoT) Market

The global Internet of Things (IoT) market was valued at US$ 190.0 Bn in 2018 and is projected to reach US$ 1102.6 Bn by 2026, exhibiting a CAGR of 24.7% during the forecast period. IoT encompasses advanced equipment such as sensors, meters, network connectivity, smart devices, and software that facilitate the interchange of information between machines and devices.

What is PRISM?

PRISM stands for Programmable Responsive Intuitive Sentient Mesh Networks. It is an AI connectivity platform designed for the creation and control of large-scale IoT networks worldwide. The software integrates with IoT devices to remotely control them wirelessly over the internet.

Key features of PRISM include:

  • Self-healing, decentralized, scalable IoT mesh networks
  • Minimal maintenance required
  • Patent-protected technology (US patent #: 10459416 B1)

PRISM AI Features

  • Real-time data exchange between data points or sensors
  • Support for billions of IoT devices, inside or outside the cloud
