What is PRISM?

PRISM stands for Programmable Responsive Intuitive Sentient Mesh Networks. It is an AI connectivity platform designed for the creation and control of large-scale IoT networks worldwide. The software integrates with IoT devices to remotely control them wirelessly over the internet.

Key features of PRISM include:

  • Self-healing, decentralized, scalable IoT mesh networks
  • Minimal maintenance required
  • Patent-protected technology: US Patent #10459416 B1
